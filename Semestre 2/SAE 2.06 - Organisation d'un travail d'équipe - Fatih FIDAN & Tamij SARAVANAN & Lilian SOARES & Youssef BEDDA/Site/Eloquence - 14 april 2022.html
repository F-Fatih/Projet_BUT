The complete replay in HD:

You will find on this page the complete replay of the eloquence contest of April 14, 2022 with the theme :

"The metaverse, real life is elsewhere?"

You will also find debriefs with some participants who were willing to give us some of their time.

These debriefs are broken down into 3 steps:
-How did they approach the subject 
-How did they feel when they were there 
-A discussion about the event in general

David

David ANTOINE

Member of the group Draco in BUT Informatique, David opened the ball by showing us a positive but addictive metaverse. 

We find a rather intimate text where we are plunged into fragile thoughts showing how the metaverse can so easily impact us.

His speeches interpreting two different characters with two different points of view moved the audience from the start.

Teva

Teva PAUZIE

Member of the group Cygnus in BUT Informatique, Teva explains that this metaverse is only a bridge between the real and the unreal.

The metaverse has the power to help everyone because it impacts all areas. This world can be a way to express your desires and be who you want to be.

Quentin

Quentin VERMEERSCH

Member of the group Pegasus in BUT Informatique, Quentin puts himself in the shoes of a desperate man who wants to escape our filthy world.

A dreamer wishes for a world where he would be happy, where he could live like a god. The metaverse would allow him to avoid this awful world.


Youssef

Youssef BEDDA

Member of the group Draco, Youssef announces a metaverse as a solution to today's problems.

In our obsolete world, gangrenous by diseases and social differences, wouldn't it be judicious to create a new one where all this would not exist? Where everyone can be whoever they want to be without worrying about prejudices and discriminations? Maybe the metaverse is the solution.

Tinhinane

Tinhinane SALEMKOUR

Member of the group Andromeda in BUT Informatique, Tinhinane warns about the dangers of metavers.

Discord, frustration and dehumanization are what will happen in our society if we let the metaverse take its place in it.

Kais

Kais HAMMACHE

Member of the group Andromeda in BUT Informatique, Kais is a lawyer ready to defend life against the usurper: the metaverse.

It denounces the perversity and the drifts of the accused because the metaverse tries to replace the real life by confusing the fictional with the real.

Ony

Ony ANDRIATSAHAVOJAONA

Member of the group Pegasus in BUT Informatique, Ony unveiled a multifaceted metaverse that creates wealth.

Different worlds so fantastic that the human mind becomes dependent on the simulator. A fracture between ideal and reality, the metaverse is only disillusionment. 

Sylla

Sylla DIABARA

Member of the group Phoenix in BUT Informatique Sylla discussed his vision of the metaverse with humor.

He approaches with ease the social aspect as well as the drifts of the metaverse, he explains us how this technology manages to lock us so much in this world where everyone gives himself a false image and avoids his responsibilities.

Result of the competition

Below, the announcement of the results after deliberation of the jury.
This year the level was very high to the point where it was impossible to define a single winner as all were so deserving! 

This is why, for the first time in the history of the BUT Informatique eloquence contests in Villetaneuse, there is no one but two first places !
