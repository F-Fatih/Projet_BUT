Program of the day

Movement in the university !

On the program of this day, the University Sorbonne Paris Nord has planned many surprises!

Between the artists who will set the mood in the forum, the writers' stands in the associations' hallway as well as the eloquence contest, there is no reason to be bored!

Meeting with artists

You will find in the main forum various musicians and singers who will take turns throughout the day to set the mood!

Not far away, in the corridor of associations, stands dedicated to the meeting between writers and students will be set up.

The eloquence contest

In theamphitheatre 7, the largest amphitheatre of the university, the great eloquence contest of the day will take place! 

Introduction to chess

To conclude the program of this busy day, you will find in the IUT room S103-S105 Volunteers from the university chess club ready to teach you the rules of chess.
