Welcome to our website !

University Sorbonne Paris Nord is organizing a great day on eloquence on Friday June 17, 2022! This site was conceived to present you the principle and the interest of this day. At the same time you will find on this site the report, the interviews as well as the photos and videos of the previous eloquence contest which took place on April 14, 2022 within the computer science department of the IUT of Villetaneuse.

But what is an eloquence contest? 

An eloquence contest is an oratory art, an ability to express oneself easily in front of an audience, using both emotions and argumentation. On the substance, the speaker is able to persuade thanks to a well constructed argument. In terms of form, he or she will use the codes of non-verbal communication, gestures, looks and silences, to convince the audience.

The speaker is invited to take the floor with his own words and personalityIt is not a question of playing a role, as it can be in the theater. Moreover, eloquence contests allow to develop confidence and to acquire a certain ease in public. It also allows you to learn to manage your stress, to overcome your shyness and thus to approach the oral in a different way.
